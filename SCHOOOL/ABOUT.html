ABOUT OUR SCHOOL


Rosmini is a core boarding school, having ordinary level and Advanced level. It is located in
Tanga. It is located 5km from the city.Rosmini as a school from the founding members The Rosminian
fathers which is a Catholic institute from Italy, having invested into the Saruji Schools and
developing them into Rosminian schools.

LEVELS OF EDUCATION WE OFFER


As stated above, we provide Ordianry and Advanced level education with the same treatment and ensuring
high performance of both Ordinary (o level) and Advanced level (A-level).
Our school enrolls more than 500 new students each year,approximately 400 of them being boys and the rest pleed
to be girls, This is because we are in shortage of girls facilities as in domitories or sleeping halls.
Also, Our school uses NECTA syllabus in academic teachings and not cambridge version.

STAFF SUMMARY AND SUBJECTS TAUGHT

Our school has a total of 65 staff members, 45 being teaching staff and 20 being non-teaching staff.
We provide a wide platform for our students by teaching a variety of subjects for the students to have
a wide range of choices.
In Ordinary level we teach Mathematices, English , Kiswahili, Geography, History, Civics, computer
and commerce.
In Advance level we have the following combinations; PCB, PCM, CBG, EGM ,HGL , HKL

VISION OF OUR SCHOOL

Our vision is "To embrace a holistic approach in the provision of education to learners".

MISSION OF OUR SCHOOL

Our mission is to provide appropriate education to the learners with a view of preparing them
academically, socially , spiritually and physically so as to make them responsible Young people
beneficial to the society.
